FAQ

How does anonymization preserve results?
Identity edits are applied once and locked across both panels. Treatment effects (from notes or defaults) are preserved without exaggeration.
Do you store my originals?
Originals are processed for the current job and deleted after processing.
What about HIPAA?
Face2 is HIPAA-minded. The Beautify should be ONLY used for consented images. The Anonymize feature replaces identity and avoids PHI exposure in outputs.
