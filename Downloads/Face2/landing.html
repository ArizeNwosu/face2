FAQ

How does anonymization preserve results?
Identity edits are applied once and locked across both panels. Treatment effects (from notes or defaults) are preserved without exaggeration.
Do you store my originals?
Originals are processed for the current job. Your account can choose whether to retain or purge originals after processing.
What about HIPAA?
Face2 is HIPAA-minded. Beautify requires consent on file; Anonymize replaces identity and avoids PHI exposure in outputs.
