1. Purpose of This Policy

Face2 ("the Service") is provided by Social Gear Media. Our platform enables med spa and aesthetics professionals to process clinical before-and-after images in two modes:

  • Beautify Mode (Consent Required) — Enhances existing patient photos for clarity, consistency, and professional presentation.
  • Anonymize Mode (Consent Not Required) — Replaces personally identifiable facial features with anonymized equivalents while preserving treatment results.

2. Consent Requirements

  • Beautify Mode may only be used on photos where you have obtained valid, written patient authorization for use, in compliance with HIPAA, state privacy laws, and your local regulations.
  • Anonymize Mode is designed to produce non-identifiable images. Because these images cannot reasonably be linked back to an individual, they do not constitute Protected Health Information (PHI) under HIPAA. As such, additional patient consent is not typically required for anonymized and unrecognizable outputs. However, you are ultimately responsible for determining if the final output is unrecognizable enough from the original.

3. User Responsibilities

By using Face2, you agree that:

  • You will not upload or process images without proper consent and/or permission.
  • You remain responsible for obtaining and storing patient authorizations in accordance with applicable laws.
  • You understand that Social Gear Media provides tools to reduce compliance risk but does not act as your legal counsel or remove risk.

4. Data Handling & Security

  • All uploaded images are processed in a stateless environment: the system uses only the original inputs for each job, then purges working memory.
  • Outputs (final anonymized or beautified composites) and job logs are stored securely by Social Gear Media for your account records.
  • We do not sell, share, or license patient images or metadata to third parties.

5. Authorization Language for Your Practice

We provide this optional authorization text for your patient intake forms (if using Beautify Mode):

Authorization for Use of Images

I authorize [Practice Name] to use my photographic images for the purposes of demonstrating treatment results in marketing, advertising, and educational materials. I understand these images may be processed using Face2 by Social Gear Media for clarity and presentation. I understand that my identity will remain visible in beautified outputs. I may revoke this authorization in writing at any time, but revocation will not affect any use already made in reliance on my authorization.

6. Limitation of Liability

Social Gear Media provides Face2 "as is." While our anonymization tools are designed to meet HIPAA de-identification standards, it is your responsibility to verify compliance in your jurisdiction. We disclaim liability for misuse of the Service, failure to obtain required consents, or non-compliant use.
