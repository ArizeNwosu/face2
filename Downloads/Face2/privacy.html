1. Introduction

Social Gear Media ("we," "our," "us") operates Face2, a web-based platform that allows med spas and aesthetics professionals to process before-and-after treatment photos through two modes:

  • Beautify Mode – enhances the quality and presentation of clinical photos (requires documented patient consent).
  • Anonymize Mode – generates de-identified before/after sets that remove or replace personal identity features, making the subject non-identifiable (consent not required).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you provide to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Face2:

  1. Account Information: Name, email, company name, and billing details when you register or subscribe.
  2. Uploaded Images: Photos that you submit for processing. These are used only for the job requested and handled according to the rules below.
  3. Job Metadata: Non-identifiable technical details of each anonymization/beautify job, such as timestamp, job ID, mode selected, variation settings, and system logs.
  4. Device & Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, session duration, and basic analytics (e.g., error logs, performance metrics).

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, process, and improve the Face2 service.
  • Ensure compliance with applicable privacy rules (e.g., requiring consent for Beautify mode).
  • Maintain internal security, debugging, and system integrity.
  • Communicate with you regarding service updates, billing, or support.
  • Conduct anonymized analytics to improve performance and usability.

We do not sell or license patient images or identifiable data to third parties.

4. Image Processing & Storage

  • Stateless Processing: Each job uses only the original images you upload. The system does not re-use previously generated outputs.
  • Temporary In-Memory Use: Images are processed in real time, then purged from working memory after completion.
  • Output Storage: Your anonymized or beautified outputs, along with the JSON job logs, are stored securely in your account area.
  • Retention: We retain outputs and logs for as long as your account is active, or until you delete them. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Consent Rules

  • Beautify Mode: You are required to obtain and maintain valid, written patient consent before uploading images. You confirm this by checking the consent box before using Beautify.
  • Anonymize Mode: Designed to produce images that are not reasonably identifiable as Protected Health Information (PHI). Consent is not required for anonymized results.

6. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information only in these cases:

  • Service Providers: With vendors that provide infrastructure (e.g., secure cloud hosting, payment processing). These providers act under contractual privacy obligations.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, regulation, subpoena, or court order.
  • Business Transfers: If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

7. Security

We employ industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted storage of job outputs, and strict role-based access. While no system is completely secure, we continuously update and monitor our defenses.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your account information.
  • Request that we restrict or object to processing.
  • Request a copy of your data in portable format.

You can exercise these rights by emailing support@face2.app.

9. Children's Privacy

Face2 is intended for business use only and is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date. If changes are significant, we will notify you via email or within the app.
